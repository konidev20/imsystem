Here is some of the basic functionality offered by the application :

  • The manager can keep track of all the products and stock in the center.
  • The manager can also keep track of the orders that have been placed and old orders.
  • Customers can place orders to stock their own retail shops with products.
  • Customers can keep track of their orders and order history.
