Inventory Management System

for a Shipping Center.

Welcome, to the Inventory Management System

This is a simple inventory management system application designed to keep track of the products stocked in the center. The following is the basic functionality of the application:

  • Customers can place orders for products.
  • Customers can track the orders they have placed.
  • Managers can keep track of the orders that have been placed.
  • Managers can keep track of the products that have been stocked in the shipping center.
  • Managers can add or remove orders, customers, products etc.
